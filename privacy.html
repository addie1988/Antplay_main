Company Profile

AntPlay N.V., Curaçao (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "our") complies with the Curaçao Personal Data Protection Act (2013). This Privacy Policy is issued on behalf of the AntPlay N.V., Curaçao group of companies, which includes the website

https://antplay.com/.

The data controller for https://antplay.com/ is AntPlay N.V., Curaçao. You may contact us with any inquiries.

Changes to This Privacy Statement

The latest update to this version is dated January 1, 2025. If you wish to obtain previous versions, please contact AntPlay N.V., Curaçao.

It is important that the personal data we hold about you be accurate and up to date. If your personal data change at any time during your relationship with us, please inform us.

  • 1. How We Use Your Personal Data

    We will only use your data for lawful purposes. Most commonly, we will use your personal data under the following circumstances:

    1. Where it is necessary for the performance of a contract we are about to enter into or have already entered into with you.
    2. Where it is necessary for our legitimate interests (or those of a third party), and your interests and fundamental rights do not override those interests.
    3. Where it is necessary to comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

  • 2. Data We Collect About You

    Personal data (or personal information) means any information about an individual from which that person can be identified. We may collect, use, store, and transfer different kinds of personal data about you, which we have grouped as follows:

    1. Identity Data: Includes first name, maiden name, last name, username or similar identifier, date of birth, and gender.
    2. Contact Data: Includes billing address, delivery address, email address, and telephone numbers.
    3. Financial Data: Includes bank account details, payment card information, and credit ratings.
    4. Technical Data: Includes internet protocol (IP) address, login data, browser type and version, etc.

  • 3. Purposes for Which We Collect Your Personal Data

    We collect and process your personal data for various purposes, including:

    1. To register you as a new customer and provide our gambling services.
    2. To process and monitor your transactions.
    3. To manage our relationship with you.
    4. To enable you to participate in promotions and competitions.
    5. To administer and protect our business and website.

  • 4. With Whom We May Share Your Data

    Your data may be shared with:

    1. Third parties for fraud detection and control purposes.
    2. Other AntPlay N.V., Curaçao group companies.
    3. Cloud services providers and data centers.
    4. Payment services and banks.
    5. We will take all reasonable steps to ensure your personal data is treated securely and in accordance with applicable laws.

  • 5. Data Retention Period

    To comply with our legal, financial, and regulatory obligations, we retain your information for the minimum period required by such obligations.

    1. The retention period will follow the relevant regulations in Curaçao (at least 10 years).
    2. If you request the erasure of your data and that request meets our deletion criteria, your personal data will be anonymized.
    3. If there is no account activity, and we have retained your data for the minimum period required by law and regulation, your account will be closed and pseudonymized.

  • 6. Your Rights

    Subject to certain limitations, you have the following rights relating to your information:

    1. Right of Access: You have the right to request access to your personal data and supplementary information.
    2. Right to Rectification: You have the right to request the correction or completion of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
    3. Right to Erasure: You have the right to request the erasure of your personal data where there is no longer a compelling reason for us to continue processing it.
    4. Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request the personal data you have provided to us.

  • 7. Cookie Policy

    The AntPlay N.V., Curaçao website uses Cookies and similar technologies to manage login sessions, provide personalized web pages, and tailor content to your specific needs and interests.

    Types of Cookies we use:

    1. Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website's basic functions.
    2. Performance Cookies: Help us improve website performance.
    3. Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and choices.
    4. Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant content and advertisements.
