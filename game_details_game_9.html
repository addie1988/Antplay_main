Game Mechanics

Every elimination is the beginning of the next surprise! Each winning symbol will be automatically eliminated, and there is a chance to randomly generate WILD, so that the connection opportunities continue to extend and stack. The bonus after elimination is multiplied by the cumulative multiplier, and the multiplier is multiplied by X2 each time it is eliminated! After entering the free game, the cumulative multiplier will not be reset, so the game process will become more and more intense, and the winning points will be fully detonated!
