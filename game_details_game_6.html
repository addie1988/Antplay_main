Game Mechanics

Multiplier evolution, non-stop winning! Whenever a WILD Symbol appears in a winning combination, it will trigger the "Multiplier +1" and move randomly. If there are multiple WILDs in the same winning combination, the winning multiplier can be multiplied. With the continuous explosion mechanism, the bonus can be instantly magnified, further boosting the sense of winning, and the score stacking is even more exciting!
