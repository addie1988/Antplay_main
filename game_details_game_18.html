Game Mechanics

The continuous elimination frenzy is coming! The game is equipped with a continuous elimination mechanism, which allows you to continue eliminating after connecting. When entering the free game, the multiplier can be stacked each time you eliminate. There is also a full-round WILD design that greatly increases the chance of winning points. It is possible to spin out a whole row of high-prize combinations in one spin!
